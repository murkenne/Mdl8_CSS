Healthy Sources of The Three Macronutrients

Explore the benefits of each macronutrient and their sources.

Protein

Proteins are essential for muscle repair, immune function, and hormone production.
Good sources include lean meats, fish, eggs, and legumes.

Fats

Fats provide long-term energy, support cell growth, and help absorb certain nutrients.
Healthy options include avocados, nuts, and olive oil.

Carbohydrates

Carbohydrates are the body's primary energy source. Complex carbs like whole grains, fruits, and vegetables are the best options.
